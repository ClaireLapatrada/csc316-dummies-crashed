Insight 1
Scarborough shows some of the highest collision counts throughout the year. It remains one of the more dangerous areas in the city, yet it still is under-addressed compared to how often incidents happen there.
Insight 2
Measures like rumble strips, anti-icing technology, improved street lighting, and dynamic speed signs are strong options for reducing collisions. However, most of these upgrades suggestions are focused in downtown Toronto, which seems to get a lot of benefit form it.
